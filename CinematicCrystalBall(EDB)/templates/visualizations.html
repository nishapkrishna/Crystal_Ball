Exploring Movie Data with Interactive Visualizations



Summary

3D Plot of ROI by Genre over Time

During our first phase of film data analysis we uncovered a number of interesting historical facts and trends with respect to the movie industry, some of which were not evident until we could visualize the data. Visualizations, such as this 3D bar plot, allowed us to better glean insight from the data.

What we see here is a rendering of film ROI by genre over time. With one look we can see that 2007 was an incredibly lucrative year for horror.

Other notable visualizations included the following:


  • Pie chart: Genre Release Percentage (all time)
  • Documentary Popularity over Time
  • ROI for Genre (for all time)
  • Adventure Popularity over Time
